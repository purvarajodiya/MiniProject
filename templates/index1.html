Our Story

Farmers and Horticulturalists are the backbone of the global supply chain. Without a stable and succesful agricultural driving force in every nation, the lives of everyone will be upended. And that is not a hypothetical. Due to climate change, disease and other microbiological factors, and many socio-economic factors, farming communities have been ravaged. Crop yields have been unpredictable, and the rates of preventable diseases have been on the rise. This is where FarmAssist comes in.

FarmAssist is your one stop destination for Horticulture and Agricultural safety. Use our product to run a health diagnosis on your Flora and our service will detect any diseases or pests that may be affecting your plants. We also provide a chat service with our experts along with pricing options in ranges of fertilizers in organic and chemical options.
